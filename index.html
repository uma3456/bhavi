Marina Beach

i have visited in 2019

Marina Beach is a natural urban beach in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, along the Bay of Bengal. The beach runs from near Fort St. George in the north to Foreshore Estate in the south, a distance of 6.0 km, making it the longest natural urban beach in the country. The beach is the first longest beach in India and the second longest in the world. The length of the beach is 13 km.
